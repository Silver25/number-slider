Description:
The Number Slider puzzle consists of a frame with numbered square blocks arranged randomly, leaving one block empty. The objective is to slide the blocks around by using the empty space to arrange them in numerical order.

Purpose:
The game challenges your problem-solving skills and logical thinking. By rearranging the numbered tiles, you aim to achieve the desired order – from least to greatest, left to right, and top to bottom12. It’s a fun way to enhance your puzzle-solving abilities and numerical understanding!
